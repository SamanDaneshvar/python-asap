About The Founder

Saman Daneshvar is a Machine Learning, Data Science, and NLP Engineer based in Ottawa, Canada. He is a software engineer, passionate about Computer Science education and computer programming.

Saman has a Master's degree in Computer Science from University of Ottawa. He speaks English and Persian, and is fluent in Python, Java, JavaScript, C, C++, R, MATLAB, and more.

He founded Python ASAP with the mission to spread the knowledge of coding and computer programming. Python ASAP aims to improve coding skills of beginners as well as experts.
